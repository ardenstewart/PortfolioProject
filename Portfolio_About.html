Who is Arden?

Some fun facts! Arden is a Wisconsin state champion in both cross country and public speaking. She has conversed with 4 US president and one queen. The name Arden itself comes from the town in Scotland where her great-grandparents met. Arden has bicycled in 38 of the 50 United States. While an avid lover of home improvement projects, going to Home Depot is her least favorite thing in the world.
